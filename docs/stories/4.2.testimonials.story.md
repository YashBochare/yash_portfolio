## Story
**As a** Visitor,
**I want** to read testimonials from colleagues or clients,
**so that** I can trust the developer's soft skills and reliability.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. Slider/Carousel functionality (auto-play).
2. Manual navigation (arrows or dots).
3. Testimonial card design (Quote, Author, Role).
4. Responsive (1 card visible on mobile, maybe 2 on wide screens, or just 1 centered).
